# Tableau-Sales-Dashboard
This is my academic project completed in August 2025

# Dashboard 1- SALES INSIGHTS OF RETAIL PARK
This dashboard provides a complete sales analysis of the retail park by presenting total sales and total orders, quantity sold across categories, year-wise sales trends by shipping mode, sales share across cities, number of orders by sales channel, and total sales across product categories, helping to evaluate sales performance and support better business decisions.

# Dashboard 2- PROFIT ANALYSIS OF RETAIL PARK
This dashboard provides a complete profit analysis of the retail park by showcasing overall profit and profit margin, top profitable products, year-wise profit trends by customer segments, category-wise profit contribution, and discount price versus profit per unit, helping to evaluate business performance and support better decision-making.
